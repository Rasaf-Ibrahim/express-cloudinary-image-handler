### Cloudinary Credentials
<PAGE_HEADING_BOTTOM/>

Before diving into the code, it's essential to set up your Cloudinary account to obtain the necessary credentials. 

<HALF_REM_SPACER___COMPONENT/>

Visit [Cloudinary's website](https://cloudinary.com/) to create an account. Once your account is set up, you can have the necessary credentials: "Cloud Name", "API Key", and "API Secret".

<HALF_REM_SPACER___COMPONENT/>

To keep your credentials secure, store them in a `.env` file in the root of your project:

``` copy
CLOUDINARY_CLOUD_NAME=your_cloud_name
CLOUDINARY_API_KEY=your_api_key
CLOUDINARY_API_SECRET=your_api_secret
```
